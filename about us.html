About Us

OreSolve Consulting and Technologies (Pty) Ltd is based in Windhoek, Namibia and provides consulting services and technology-based solutions.

The purpose of the organisation is to support business operations, enable better decision-making, and provide project management services in implementing sustainable solutions in an ever-changing.

The organisation's focus areas are: project management, health systems strengthening, research, monitoring and evaluation, business and patient treatment process design and re-engineering, systems development and ICT support, accounting and administration, human resources, and payroll administration.

Core Values

  • Innovation: Pioneering sustainable solutions that address ever-changing challenges.
  • Integrity: Upholding the highest ethical standards in all our endeavours.
  • Collaboration: Fostering partnerships for collective growth.
  • Excellence: Delivering unwavering quality in every service and product.
  • Elasticity: Change catalysts who always find a way forward.
